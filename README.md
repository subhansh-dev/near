# SUBHANSH

a personal site built from scratch with html, css, and js.

dark grunge aesthetic. piano keys. floating images that follow your cursor. a glitch effect on the name. a mini piano you can actually play (click the keys or use your keyboard: a s d f g h j k for white keys, w e t y u for black keys). a terminal in the contact section that types itself out. parallax particles in the hero. scroll-triggered reveals. tilt effects on cards and images. a custom cursor with a trailing dot. animated stat counters. a music visualizer.

## sections

- hero with parallax floating images, typewriter tagline, and particle canvas
- about me with tilt image, scanline overlay, and animated stat counters
- hobbies with draggable windows and descriptions
- projects with gradient hover effects
- music with visualizer bars, mini piano, and spotify embed
- gallery with grid layout and hover overlays
- contact with social links, terminal animation, and background image

## keyboard shortcuts

on the mini piano:
- white keys: a s d f g h j k
- black keys: w e t y u

## built with

- html
- css (custom properties, grid, animations, backdrop-filter, clip-path)
- vanilla js (intersection observer, web audio api, canvas, requestAnimationFrame)
